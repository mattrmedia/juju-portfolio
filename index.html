about me

Design is all about problem-solving, putting together the pieces of the puzzle until you reach a harmonious result, feeling that satisfaction when it all comes together. Whether it's combining efficiency and quality, or aesthetics and functionality, I strive to always achieve that balance. I pride myself on attention to detail, nudging down to the last pixel and catching those pesky double spaces before they reach the printer.

On a more personal note, I love hiking and backpacking, collecting collections, doing agility with my dog, and am always down for bar trivia. I enjoy visiting new places and trying restaurants featured on Diners, Drive-ins, and Dives whenever possible.
